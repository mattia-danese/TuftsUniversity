How The App Works


With the SteerMyEvent Web App, you can browse through our catalog of partner venues, choose the one that best meets your needs based on amenities, reviews, size, and pricing, and tentatively reserve it for your event.


Once we contact the restaurant to confirm your booking, someone from our team will reach out to you to start planning the finer details of your event, such as decor, the food and drinks menu, and more!
